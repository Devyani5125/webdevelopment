🌐 Google

Google is one of the most widely used search engines in the world. It allows users to search for information, images, videos, news, and more on the internet.

Google was founded in 1998 by Larry Page and Sergey Brin while they were PhD students at Stanford University.

The main aim of Google is to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful.

Google is a part of Alphabet Inc. and offers a wide range of services and products.

Some popular services provided by Google are:

  • Gmail – Email service
  • Google Maps – Navigation and location services
  • YouTube – Video sharing platform
  • Google Drive – Cloud storage
  • Google Chrome – Web browser
