The Alchemist

By Paulo Coelho

The story follows Santiago, a young shepherd boy from Spain who dreams about finding a treasure near the Egyptian pyramids.
Believing the dream is a sign, he decides to leave his comfortable life and search for the treasure.

During his journey:
He travels from Spain to Africa
Loses his money but learns important life lessons
Works for a crystal merchant
Meets an Englishman studying alchemy
Travels across the desert
Meets an alchemist who teaches him to listen to his heart
